Simple process

Request first, confirm next.

Simple pricing example

About half of the final checkout price.

CineBuddy looks at the final ticket price shown at checkout, including booking fees and taxes. The discounted price is usually calculated as half of that final amount, then rounded to the nearest dollar. Half-dollar values round up.

Example
$18.56 $9

$18.56 ÷ 2 = $9.28, then rounded to $9.

$15.20 $8

$15.20 ÷ 2 = $7.60, rounded to nearest dollar.

$21.40 $11

$21.40 ÷ 2 = $10.70, rounded to nearest dollar.

$24.99 $13

$24.99 ÷ 2 = $12.50, half-dollar rounds up.
