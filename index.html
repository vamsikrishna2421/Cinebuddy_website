Simple process

Request first, confirm next.

CineBuddy is a request-based service. It is not a fully automated ticket booking platform, so every order gets a human review before payment and ticket details are finalized.

1

Movie, Regal theater, date, showtime, ticket count, and contact details.

Simple pricing example

About half of the final checkout price.

CineBuddy looks at the final ticket price shown at checkout, including booking fees and taxes. The discounted price is usually calculated as half of that final amount, then rounded up to the nearest dollar.

Example
$18.56 $10

$18.56 ÷ 2 = $9.28, then rounded up to $10.

$15.20 $8

$15.20 ÷ 2 = $7.60, rounded up.

$21.40 $11

$21.40 ÷ 2 = $10.70, rounded up.

$24.99 $13

$24.99 ÷ 2 = $12.50, rounded up.
